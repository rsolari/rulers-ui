# Glossary of Terms

**Agents** — Agents of the Crown. When a Noble is sent to perform Actions on behalf of a Ruler.

**Army** — A collection of military Troops outside of a Settlement.

**Building** — A structure that provides a benefit to a Realm.

**Character** — Can refer to a Ruler, Realm or Significant Individual. A Player's character is both the Ruler and the Realm.

**Garrison** — A collection of military Troops stationed in a Settlement or Building.

**Industry** — Generates Wealth from Resources.

**Money** — The currency in game used to Construct Buildings, Recruit Troops and perform other Actions.

**Narrative Overlord** — The game's master or "referee". They are in charge of the game's narrative, controlling everything Players do not, and ensuring everyone has fun. The N.O. cannot also be a Player.

**Noble** — An NPC Character who commands Settlements, Armies and can act as Agents.

**NPC** — Non-Player Character, i.e. a character that is not directly controlled by a Player.

**Player** — The people who are playing a game of Rulers!

**Products** — Goods produced by Industries.

**Realm** — The total Territory governed by a Ruler.

**Resource** — Either Common or Luxury. Determines the base Wealth generated by the Industries associated with it.

**Roleplay** — Acting out and playing an imaginary character or persona.

**Ruler** — The Character who governs an entire Realm.

**Season** — Each turn in Rulers reflects the passage of one season of the year (Spring, Summer, Autumn, Winter).

**Settlement** — Centres of Industries, Wealth generation, Food Production and homes for the populace.

**Taxes** — The means by which a Ruler receives an income from the Settlements of a Realm.

**Territory** — The borders on the map that make areas which can be governed.

**Trade** — When two or more Realms have a Trade Route.

**Troop** — A group of military units.

**Upkeep** — The cost of paying for Troops, Nobles and Buildings every Season.

**Value** — How much something in Rulers is worth.

**Wealth** — Money generated by a Realm's economy of Industries and Trade.
