# Character Creation

In a game of Rulers, a Player creates a Realm and a Ruler. The Realm is a civilisation and its territory. This includes its borders, its races, faith, language, armaments, clothes, architecture and culture. The Ruler is the individual character who governs the Realm. Your Ruler is your Lord, Duke, King, Governor, Magnate or Magistrate — whatever you want to call them. They preside over your Realm.

## Realm Creation Checklist

- What are your resources?
- Choose a form of Government
- Choose your Traditions
- Create your Ruler
- Create your Noble Families
- Starting Army
- Create your Guilds/Orders/Societies
- Add the details to your Realm

Once all players have created their Realm and their Ruler, take a look at what is similar in each Realm. Do other Realms have similar Races, Language or Faith? How can the world be better explained by expanding on the relationships between Realms? Maybe your people are Refugees from another Realm, outcast heretics, or maybe they are the heretics!

Rulers and Realms always benefit from not having a stand-alone-complex. Remember, the world the N.O. and the Players have created is likely to have housed these civilisations for centuries, so they are bound to have a complex history of inter-cultural integration and opposition. Build on that, and constantly build on your lore!
