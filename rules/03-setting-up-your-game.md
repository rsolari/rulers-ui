# Setting Up Your Game

To set up your game, make sure both the Players and the N.O. have done the following:

- Created a World filled with Resources
- Created a shared history for your World
- All Players have a completed Character (Realm, Ruler, Nobles, Guilds, Starting Army, Settlements placed and named, income from Taxes and Upkeep calculated)
- All existing Realms have been granted territory on the World Map
- There is a World Map and a map for each Player Realm

## What You Need to Play

- A World Map
- d10s, d8s and d6s (10, 8 and 6 sided dice respectively)
- Character Sheets
- Players and an N.O.

We recommend no more than 6 players, but no less than 3.[^1] The N.O. cannot be one of these players. Every Player must have completed Character Creation and have both a Ruler and Realm. The N.O. may do this for NPCs at their discretion. Sometimes numbers and dice appropriate to the circumstances are all that are needed.

[^1]: Neither shalt thou hast 2 players, unless of course, thoust is preceding to 3. 7, is right out!

## What You Start With (the Players)

- 3 Common Resources
- 1 Luxury Resource
- 5 Settlements: 4 Villages and 1 Town — one at each Resource site and one free-standing Settlement
- A Standing Army of 5 Troops
- A Tax Rate of: Low

Calculate the total Wealth for the whole Realm, your income from your Tax rate, and total up your upkeep. A Player's Realm starts with 5 Troops that the Realm is capable of recruiting. These Troops have to be Garrisoned in Settlements.

## Starting Play!

In the opening session, the N.O. should introduce the NPC Realms and the current political situation, as well as the world. We then suggest giving each Player an opportunity to describe their Ruler, and let other Players know what would be common knowledge about their Realm, its culture, and its people. This first session can be done over a voice calling service, or in person. It is up to you if you want to conduct future sessions in this manner, or just send in the Reports.
