# Turmoil

Nobody likes having Taxes levied upon them, especial - ly those who
are struggling to feed their families and scratch out a living. And
these same people can be pretty terrifying when armed with pitchforks
and torches! Turmoil represents unrest in your Realm. It can come from
Taxation, Food Shortages, War, Disasters or just from the natural
obnoxiousness of the Ruler. As Turmoil rises in your Realm, the greater
the chances are of the people rioting or rebelling. Nobles who aren't
fond of your rule, may seize such an opportunity to over - throw the
Ruler. Calculating Turmoil Turmoil can come from a number of different
sources, but all ultimately fuel disorder and tension in the Realm,
across all social ranks. While Turmoil can raise to unknown heights of
discon - tent, it is not possible for Turmoil to become a negative
number or in any way create a positive sentiment in your Realm. Taxes
