# Making Money

Rulers is first and foremost a storytelling game of politics and nation-building. However, a [Ruler](12-creating-your-ruler.md) cannot improve the prosperity of their subjects without Wealth. Wealth is created from [Resources](08-territories-and-resources.md), [Industries](24-resources-and-industries.md), [Trade](25-trade.md) and [Food](20-food-for-the-people.md). As products are sold and traded within the Realm, and across borders, Wealth is generated. Wealth can be Taxed and this is how a Ruler fills their Treasury.

With these funds they can raise an [Army](27-armies.md), construct [Buildings](26-buildings.md), invest in what they see fit, and pay for the Upkeep of the Realm and its [Nobles](16-nobles.md). Largely, people are smart enough to manage their own businesses. The role of a Ruler is not to meddle in the daily work of their subjects, but instead to protect and expand Trade, acquire Resources, and invest in projects that no individual community could afford on their own.

## Tribute & Taxation

**Tribute** is what is paid to the Ruler to maintain the Realm. **Taxes** can be levied to increase the funds coming into the Treasury, but may be unpopular.

Tribute to the Ruler is their primary source of income. These are the funds that are used to pay for the construction of Buildings, the Upkeep of the Realm's Nobles, the recruitment of [Troops](27-armies.md), as well as certain [Political Actions](06-actions-in-rulers.md#political-actions).

The Tribute paid in Taxes to the Ruler of the Realm is:

> **15% of the Wealth from each [Settlement](19-settlements.md)**, paid every Season.

This does not decrease the amount of Wealth generated. The biggest Wealth creators are Resources and their respective Industries. In addition to this, Food also produces Wealth, and there may be other sources of Wealth in the Settlement, but this is at the discretion of the N.O.

## Levying Taxes

It is possible for a Ruler to increase their income by Levying Taxes.

> **Levy Taxes = Income becomes 30% of the Wealth from each Settlement**

This action can be performed as a [Fiscal Action](06-actions-in-rulers.md#fiscal-actions) by the Ruler of a Realm. Once enacted, it remains in place for one year, at which point it can be repealed or allowed to remain in effect. However, Levy Taxes produces **10 [Turmoil](21-turmoil.md)** per Season it is in effect.

If a Player wishes to levy taxes on something specific, the N.O. may consider this: in order to be Taxed, the object in question must be creating Wealth for the Realm. Generating Wealth describes something that is being traded and circulated around the Realm's Economy.
