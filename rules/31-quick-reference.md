## [Trade](25-trade.md) & [Tax](23-making-money.md)

- **[Levy Taxes](23-making-money.md#levying-taxes):** 30% of Settlement Wealth instead

## [Warfare](29-warfare.md)

### [Quick Combat](29-warfare.md#battles--quick-combat)

- Roll 1d6 x Number of [Troops](27-armies.md) + Weapon/Armour Bonuses (same type do not stack)
- +1 dice for every 3 of the same type of unit
- Success for Basic Troops = 5–6
- Success for Elite Troops = 4–6

### [Full Combat](29-warfare.md#battles--full-combat)

- Basic Troops = 4d6
- Elite Troops = 6d6

### [Advantage Table](29-warfare.md#resolving-dice-rolls)

| Advantage | Consequence |
|---|---|
| +1–2 | Wounded |
| +3–4 | Routed |
| +5 | Defeated |
| +6 or more | Crushed |
