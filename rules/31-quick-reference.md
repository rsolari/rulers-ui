## Trade & Tax

- **Trade Routes:** +5% increase to Wealth generated by the Settlement for each Product exported
- **Taxation:** 15% of Settlement Wealth
- **Levy Taxes:** 30% of Settlement Wealth instead

## Dice Rolls

- **Nobles:** 5d6 for Cunning or Reason
- **Turmoil:** 1d6 per point of Turmoil

## Warfare

### Quick Combat

- Roll 1d6 × Number of Troops + Weapon/Armour Bonuses (same type do not stack)
- +1 dice for every 3 of the same type of unit
- Success for Basic Troops = 5–6
- Success for Elite Troops = 4–6

### Full Combat

- Basic Troops = 4d6
- Elite Troops = 6d6
