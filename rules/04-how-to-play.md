# How to Play

How To Play Each turn (one Season of game time) Players will submit a
Report to their N.O. detailing the Actions of their Ruler. This can be
done by Email, or your preferred method. Just remember that a decree of
secrecy is involved when Players take their actions. Only the N.O. has a
complete picture of the game. It is up to the N.O. to establish a time
when one turn (Season) advances. This could be every Sunday, or every
alternating Friday, at noon or at midnight. This deadline by which all
Reports should be in by is by the N.O. and they should be firm with
their Players to keep to it. The N.O. will naturally put a lot of time
and ef - fort into running the game, so the players should respect this
but getting their Reports in on time. Sending Reports Each Report should
contain the following: • Financial Actions − Any changes to Taxes − Any
Buildings Constructed − Any Troops Recruited − Any Other Money Spent •
Political Actions − Details of Any Political Actions (Max. 6) taken by
the Ruler • Game Date (e.g. Year 1, Season 1) In addition to this, the
Player can add any storytelling details to flesh out the actions taken
by the Ruler, or to give depth to the Realm, as long as they are not

Receiving Replies After all the Reports are in, it is the N.O.'s turn to
write the replies. A reply should include: • Acknowledgement of Money
spent • Replies to any Messages that were sent • A resolution to each
Political Action • Any additional Story elements the N.O. It is up to
Players to track their own Financial Actions, positions of Armies and
the progress of Buildings being constructed. Political Actions need to
be resolved by the N.O. The N.O. should detailing whether they were
successful or not, what the response was, or how they played out.1 Once
the Player Actions have been resolved, it is time for the various NPC
Realms and factions to act. The N.O. should keep notes, but only what
directly im - pacts a Player needs to be written in the reply. For
instance, an NPC Realm can Recruit Troops, and order them to Capture
another Realm's Settlement -- but the Player will only know about this
when they see them advancing into their Territory. 1 No more than a
sentence or two is necessary for each one.
