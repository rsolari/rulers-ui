## Closing

*Build an Empire. Forge your Dynasty. Rule.*

I hope the *Rulers: Core Rulebook* has inspired you to start playing a game of civilisations and empires with your friends and future political adversaries.

Originally my dream was to continue to update and expand upon Rulers. However, I have designed two more games since then, and as much as I love Rulers, if I were to return to the project I would start over. Hopefully I will do that one day, as civilisation games remain a passion of mine.

By releasing this version of Rulers under a Creative Commons license, I hope Rulers might develop its own, active community. So show me your artwork, tell me about your world, and send in your own homebrewed rules! Stay in touch!
