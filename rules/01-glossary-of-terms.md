# Glossary of Terms

**Agents** — Agents of the Crown. When a [Noble](16-nobles.md) is sent to perform [Actions](06-actions-in-rulers.md) on behalf of a [Ruler](12-creating-your-ruler.md). See [Reason and Cunning](17-reason-and-cunning.md).

**[Army](27-armies.md)** — A collection of military Troops outside of a [Settlement](19-settlements.md).

**[Building](26-buildings.md)** — A structure that provides a benefit to a Realm.

**Character** — Can refer to a [Ruler](12-creating-your-ruler.md), Realm or Significant Individual. A Player's character is both the Ruler and the Realm.

**Garrison** — A collection of military [Troops](27-armies.md) stationed in a [Settlement](19-settlements.md) or [Building](26-buildings.md).

**[Industry](24-resources-and-industries.md)** — Generates Wealth from [Resources](08-territories-and-resources.md).

**Money** — The currency in game used to Construct [Buildings](26-buildings.md), Recruit [Troops](27-armies.md) and perform other [Actions](06-actions-in-rulers.md).

**Narrative Overlord** — The game's master or "referee". They are in charge of the game's narrative, controlling everything Players do not, and ensuring everyone has fun. The N.O. cannot also be a Player.

**[Noble](16-nobles.md)** — An NPC Character who commands [Settlements](19-settlements.md), [Armies](27-armies.md) and can act as Agents.

**NPC** — Non-Player Character, i.e. a character that is not directly controlled by a Player.

**Player** — The people who are playing a game of Rulers!

**Products** — Goods produced by [Industries](24-resources-and-industries.md).

**Realm** — The total [Territory](08-territories-and-resources.md) governed by a [Ruler](12-creating-your-ruler.md). See [The Basics](05-the-basics-of-rulers.md).

**[Resource](08-territories-and-resources.md)** — Either Common or Luxury. Determines the base Wealth generated by the [Industries](24-resources-and-industries.md) associated with it.

**Roleplay** — Acting out and playing an imaginary character or persona.

**[Ruler](12-creating-your-ruler.md)** — The Character who governs an entire Realm.

**Season** — Each turn in Rulers reflects the passage of one season of the year (Spring, Summer, Autumn, Winter). See [The Basics](05-the-basics-of-rulers.md).

**[Settlement](19-settlements.md)** — Centres of [Industries](24-resources-and-industries.md), Wealth generation, [Food](20-food-for-the-people.md) Production and homes for the populace.

**[Taxes](23-making-money.md)** — The means by which a Ruler receives an income from the Settlements of a Realm.

**[Territory](08-territories-and-resources.md)** — The borders on the map that make areas which can be governed.

**[Trade](25-trade.md)** — When two or more Realms have a Trade Route.

**[Troop](27-armies.md)** — A group of military units.

**Upkeep** — The cost of paying for [Troops](27-armies.md), [Nobles](16-nobles.md) and [Buildings](26-buildings.md) every Season. See [The Basics](05-the-basics-of-rulers.md).

**Value** — How much something in Rulers is worth.

**Wealth** — Money generated by a Realm's economy of [Industries](24-resources-and-industries.md) and [Trade](25-trade.md). See [Making Money](23-making-money.md).
